Our Story

From a Vision to a Global Brand

Founded in 1998, Lamico Investment Group began with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide the purest, highest-quality drinking water to communities across the region and beyond.

What started as a small operation has grown into one of the leading water production and export companies in the region. Our journey reflects a deep commitment to quality, sustainability, and community well-being.

Today, our state-of-the-art production facilities utilize the latest in water purification and bottling technology, ensuring every drop that bears our name meets the most stringent international quality standards.
